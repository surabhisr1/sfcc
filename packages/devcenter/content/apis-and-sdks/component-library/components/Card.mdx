### Basic example

```jsx react-live=true
<Card
    header={<h1>Header</h1>}
    children={<div>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit</div>}
    footer={<div>Footer</div>}
/>
```

### With borders and shadow

```jsx react-live=true
<Card
    header={<h1>Header</h1>}
    children={<div>Children content with shadow and borders</div>}
    footer={<div>Footer</div>}
    hasShadow
    hasBorder
/>
```
