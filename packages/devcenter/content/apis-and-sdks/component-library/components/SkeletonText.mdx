<div class="component-intro">

A skeleton component which mimics lines of text on a page. Internally, it uses [SkeletonInline](SkeletonInline).

This type of skeleton component is most useful when you need to mimic multiple lines of text, such as paragraphs or blocks of information.

Related components: [SkeletonBlock](SkeletonBlock) and [SkeletonInline](SkeletonInline).

</div>
